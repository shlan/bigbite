THE BIG BITE STORY




Big Bite Deli is a family-owned and local-run sandwich deli right in the heart of El Cajon. Operated by brothers, Fanar and Farkad Katto, this sandwich deli aims to bring its customers a neighborly and friendly experience. Having 6 years of experience managing and running deli sandwich shops before, the brothers hope to bring their customers an even greater experience by utilizing what they know their customers love.

So why Big Bite Deli?

Big Bite Deli's ingredients are locally-sourced. We pride ourselves in supporting the local community while putting out the freshest ingredients. The meats and vegetables are cut daily to give our customers the best of the best.

We try and craft an experience that is like no other. All of our sandwiches are created from previous customer favorites and are constantly evolving to include new flavors that our customers like. What's more, our customers are free to build their own kind of sandwich and put their own little twist on classic menu items.
